01 · Overview

What InsightScore does

Plain English: We read LinkedIn posts on your screen, ask AI to score them, and show you the result. That's it.

InsightScore is a Chrome extension that evaluates the quality of LinkedIn posts using AI. When you scroll through your LinkedIn feed, the extension reads the visible text of each post, sends it to our scoring service, and displays a quality badge (1–10) alongside the post. You can hover over any badge to see a one-sentence verdict of what the post says and whether it's backed up.

The extension only activates on linkedin.com. It does not run on any other website, does not read your private messages, and does not access pages outside of LinkedIn.

04 · Processing

How your data flows

Plain English: Post text goes to our server → AI scores it → score comes back to your browser. Nothing is kept on the server after the response.

Authentication

InsightScore uses Google OAuth to sign you in. We receive an identity token from Google — we never see or store your Google password. This token is exchanged with our authentication provider (Supabase) to create a secure session.

Post scoring

When a LinkedIn post appears on your screen, the extension extracts its visible text and sends it to our secure proxy server (hosted on Cloudflare Workers). The proxy forwards the content to an AI model for evaluation. The AI returns a quality score and short verdict. Post content is not retained on the proxy server after the response is returned.

Storage

Scored posts are stored in your personal account in our database so you can review your history in the extension popup. Each record is tied to your user ID and is never visible to other users. Row-level security ensures you can only query your own data.

Local caching

Score results are cached locally in your browser (IndexedDB) to avoid redundant API calls when you see the same post again. This cache lives entirely on your device and automatically expires after 7 days.

09 · Security

How we protect your data

All communication between the extension, our proxy, and our database is encrypted with HTTPS/TLS. Authentication tokens are stored in Chrome's secure extension storage and are refreshed automatically on a 30-minute cycle. Our database uses row-level security policies so that every query is scoped to the authenticated user — there is no way for one user to access another user's data.
